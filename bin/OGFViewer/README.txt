OGF Viewer by Alundaio
---------------------------
Tips: 

1. You can drag *.ogf into the window instead of using the load button



--------------------------
Controls
--------------------------
Click and hold Left-mouse button to drag UI elements
Hold Right-mouse button to rotate the viewport
Hold Middle-mouse button to pan the viewport
Mouse-wheel will Zoom the viewport
Hold left-Control while panning or zooming to increment more slowly
